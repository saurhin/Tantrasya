About Tantrasya

Tantrasya — meaning “of the Tantra” — is a space born from deep reverence for the occult, mysticism, and the healing sciences of ancient traditions.

Founded by Saurhin, a clair-sentient healer and spiritual guide, Tantrasya is not just a practice — it is a calling. With years of experience in crystal healing, paranormal research, esoteric studies, and mantra sadhana, the goal is to offer seekers clarity, cleansing, and connection to their higher self.

This platform is a blend of grounded energy work and divine exploration. Whether it is through crystals, dowsing, divination, or soul guidance — you are invited to rediscover the sacred within and around you.
