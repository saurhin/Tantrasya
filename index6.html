About Tantrasya

Tantrasya — meaning “Of the Tantra” — is not just a name; it is a sacred vibration, a path carved through lifetimes of inner knowing, cosmic remembrance, and silent work done in the shadow of the unseen. I am Saurhin Parikh, a clairsentient healer, crystal intuitive, and spiritual guide—not by claim, but by calling. Tantrasya is my offering to the world. It is where sacred sciences, energy healing, ancient mantras, intuitive ritual, and spiritual integrity converge.

This is not a commercial path. It is a journey of service — of helping others reconnect with what they have lost in themselves. Whether through crystals, aura reading, mantra sadhana, chakra alignment, cord cutting, or paranormal investigations, every session is designed with reverence, not performance.

I do not promise miracles. I offer tools, insight, and guidance—but the real transformation comes when you walk the path with awareness and surrender. Tantrasya is here for those who are ready to face the truth, heal deeply, and reclaim their own power through the mysteries of tantra and energy work.

If you're seeking not just answers but understanding… not just healing but awakening… then perhaps you were meant to arrive here.
