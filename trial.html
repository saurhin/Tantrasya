Consultations & Guidelines

At Tantrasya, I do not charge for consultations. Whether it's your first message or your tenth, guidance is offered freely, with sincerity and care.

This path is not a business—it's a calling. Healing, clarity, and spiritual insight should never be limited by money or fear.

If I ever recommend a crystal or remedy, it’s completely your choice to buy them or feel free to buy from anywhere. There is no pressure, no compulsion—only what resonates with you.

You’re welcome to reach out, share your story, ask questions, or simply talk. This space is yours too, without conditions.

All consultations are given with utmost sincerity and respect to your time and journey. However, to maintain balance and protect my energy space, please note the following:

Telephonic Consultations: Kindly request a specific time slot. Random calls during the day will not be entertained. A 10-minute delay is understandable, but beyond 30 minutes, the session will be considered missed or chargeable.

I request everyone to value this time, as I too have my personal life, spiritual duties, and healing work to perform.

Thank you for understanding.

If you wish to seek help, surrender with faith, and the path will reveal itself.

Energy Exchange

The work I offer through Tantrasya is grounded in sincerity, presence, and care. While there is no fixed fee for consultations, each session carries its own depth — often involving time, energy, and unseen spiritual effort.

If you ever feel moved to offer something in return — as a gesture of appreciation or alignment — you are welcome to do so. There is no obligation, only mutual respect. What is shared here flows from a space of trust, not transaction.
